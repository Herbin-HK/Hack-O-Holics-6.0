Cyber security is a vital domain dedicated to protecting digital systems, networks, and data from malicious activities. It encompasses a wide range of strategies and technologies aimed at preventing, detecting, and responding to cyber threats such as malware, phishing, and hacking attempts.

Professionals in this field deploy robust defenses like encryption, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems to safeguard sensitive information and ensure the integrity and availability of digital assets.

As cyber threats evolve in complexity and frequency, continuous adaptation and innovation in cyber security practices are crucial to mitigate risks and maintain the trust and security of individuals, organizations, and nations worldwide.
