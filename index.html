About the Event

Hack O' Holics 6.0 is the flagship 24-hour hackathon of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at St. Joseph's Institute of Technology, organized in collaboration with the Computer Society of India (CSI) Region 7 Chapter, uniting the institute's academic strength with the professional expertise of India's premier computer society to foster real-world problem solving and industry networking for students and young professionals.
